## Objective
Build a voice-based AI assistant to support healthcare access in rural India.

## Key Requirements
- Support voice input in regional languages
- Work in low or intermittent internet connectivity
- Capture patient symptoms via voice
- Convert speech to structured medical data
- Enable remote doctor review
- Ensure data privacy and security

## Users
- Rural patients
- ASHA / health workers
- Doctors

## Constraints
- Low-cost devices
- Limited bandwidth
- Offline-first usage
